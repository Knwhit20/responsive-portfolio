About Me

My name Kristen Stroup, I'm born and raised in Austin, Texas. I graduated from the University of Texas in 2010 with my Bachelor of Science in Nursing. I've spent the last 9 years working as a critical care nurse and post anesthesia care nurse in the Austin area.

I'm married and have 2 dogs named Fitzwilliam and Maggie. I love to hike, bike, read and spend time on the lake. I love to travel to new places and experience new cultures. I recently traveled to Peru and spent 4 days hiking the Inca trail to Machu Picchu.
